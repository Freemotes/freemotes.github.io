I'm <a href="https://www.twitch.tv/gromfalloon/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Gromfalloon</a> on Twitch. When I'm not streaming games, I'm often working on emotes on my alternate account, Gromballoon! I do commissions and custom work, but I'm also focusing a lot on making free emotes to give away to the world on this website. Times are tough for a lot of people, so this is my new mission. I'm having a great time sharing!

On my main gaming account, I stream a bit of everything. My favorite game of all time is Super Metroid, and I love doing various randomizers of it. I'm also a huge fan of Portal 1 and 2, Subnautica, Super Mario RPG, NieR Automata, etc. I've been hacking away at more of the Souls games and overall been enjoying them.

What I've recently started doing and really really love, is exploring game libraries. I started off with Atari Jaguar, playing every game for an hour. This gives me a great way of getting a taste for everything without any obligations. Since then, I've gone through the libraries of Jaguar CD, Virtual Boy, and Sega 32X. I have future plans of checking out the Panasonic 3DO, Atari Lynx, and other misfit consoles.

When I'm not streaming, I'm generally spending time with my wife (who was wonderful enough to make this website for me) and cat, or watching old VHS tapes of Transformers and GI Joe.

I'm incredibly grateful for all the support I've gotten on Twitch over the past decade. I wouldn't be who I am without all the wonderful people out there helping me out. If you're reading this, then thank you as well.
